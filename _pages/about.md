I am a PhD student in the Department of Political Science at University of California, Berkeley. My research interests include conflict, political violence, authoritarian politics, and survey methodology. My ongoing research examines perceptions towards and support for violent conflict actors. My work has been published in the _Journal of Elections, Public Opinion and Parties_, _International Journal of Public Opinion Research_ and _Japanese Journal of Political Science_. 

I hold an MPhil in International Relations from University of Oxford, and a BSocSc in Politics from University of Hong Kong. Since 2026, I have founded and organized the Conflict and Violence Workshop at Berkeley for graduate students to discuss and receive feedback on related work in progress. I am also a supporting team member of the [Global Research Association of Politics in Hong Kong (GRAPH)](http://graph-hk.github.io/web). I was a freelance journalist and worked with The Economist, Novaya Gazeta, DIE ZEIT, Vice News, Berlingske, Schweizer Radio und Fernsehen (SRF), and Le Média.
